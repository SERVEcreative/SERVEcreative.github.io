Request account & data deletion

Muscle Guide lets you sign in with Google for Premium subscription. You can use the free app without an account. This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated data stored on our servers.

How to request deletion

  1. Email professional.rahul.business@gmail.com from the same Google account you used to sign in to Muscle Guide.
  2. Subject line: Muscle Guide — Delete my account
  3. We will verify your request and delete your server-side profile within 30 days.

Data that will be deleted

  • Your Supabase profile record (Google user ID, email, name, avatar URL)
  • Premium / subscription status linked to your account on our servers

Data that may be kept

  • Purchase records required by Google Play for billing, tax, or fraud prevention (managed by Google Play, not us)
  • Minimal logs retained only if required by law

Data on your device

Profile, workout rooms, and downloaded videos stored only on your device are not on our servers. Remove them anytime: Android Settings → Apps → Muscle Guide → Storage → Clear data, or uninstall the app.

Active subscriptions

Deleting your account does not cancel a Google Play subscription automatically. Cancel in Google Play → Payments & subscriptions before or after requesting deletion.
