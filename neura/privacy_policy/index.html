Overview

NEURA is a personalized recovery companion app published by Serve Creative. This policy explains what information is used when you use the app com.app1.app1. By using NEURA, you agree to this policy.

Information stored on your device

NEURA stores your recovery data locally on your device only. This may include:

  • Assessment answers and recovery plan preferences
  • Streak and progress tracking
  • Urge Shield and protection settings
  • Accountability partner preferences (contact details you enter)
  • Reminder and notification preferences

This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. We do not operate a backend that collects or stores your recovery information. Uninstalling the app removes this local data unless you have backed up your device.

Local notifications

If you enable reminders, NEURA schedules notifications on your device using local notification services. Notification content is generated on your device and is not sent to external servers for delivery.

What we do not collect

NEURA does not include advertising, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking. We do not sell your data. We do not require an account to use the app.

Permissions

  • Notifications — optional recovery reminders you configure.
  • Internet — not required for core app functionality.

Children

NEURA is intended for adults seeking recovery support. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date will change when we do. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
