भक्ति साधना (Bhakti Sadhana) Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · App: com.servecreative.bhakti_sadhana

Overview

Bhakti Sadhana is a devotional app for puja, aarti, mantras, vrat, and related content, published by Serve Creative. This policy explains what information is used when you use the app com.servecreative.bhakti_sadhana. By using Bhakti Sadhana, you agree to this policy.

Information stored on your device

The app may store preferences and usage state locally on your device (for example language settings, playback state, and cached content). This local data is not sent to our own servers unless described below.

Content delivery (Supabase)

We use Supabase to deliver app content such as live darshan temple listings, AdMob configuration, and hosted audio files (aarti). When the app fetches this content, Supabase may process standard request metadata such as IP address and device type as part of normal network operations.

We do not require you to create an account to use Bhakti Sadhana. We do not intentionally collect personal identifiers through Supabase for this app.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. Google may collect and process information such as advertising ID, device information, IP address, and ad interaction data to show and measure ads.

See Google's Privacy Policy. You can reset or limit ad personalization in your device ad settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads).

YouTube and embedded content

Live darshan and other features may load content from YouTube or display pages in an in-app browser. When you use these features, YouTube/Google may collect data according to their policies. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Text-to-speech

Katha (story) playback may use on-device text-to-speech. Speech synthesis is processed locally on your device where supported by the operating system.

Permissions

  • Internet — fetch content, ads, live streams, and updates.
  • Advertising ID — used by ad networks where applicable.
  • Keep screen on — during aarti or playback sessions.
  • Wake lock — prevent interruption during audio playback.

Children

Bhakti Sadhana is a general-audience devotional app. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy; the date above will change when we do. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
